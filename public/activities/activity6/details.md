Activity# 6 : Childrens day celebration for government school kids in Chintamani 
Date : Nov 18th 2023
Location : Batlahalli, Chintamani 
Rupee For Humanity spent 1 day with around 300 kids in government school kids in Chintamani. Overall 300 kids were supported with this event. Below are the programs : 
1.	Sports day celebration
2.	Books and stationery distribution 
3.	Awareness on waste segration management 
4.	Awareness about menstrual hygiene 
5.	Lunch and snacks distribution 
6.	Encouraging sports by giving them required sports kit for students 
7.	Donating books to library which will be used for the kids 

Event was covered and recognized by Praja vani newspaper.
