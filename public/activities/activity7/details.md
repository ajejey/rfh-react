Activity# 7 : Visit to Manava Sarathi NGO 
Date : October 21st 2023 
We from Rupee For Humanity visits all the NGO and serve them what they need at most. As this is online NGO, we support the orphanages in this way so that they can get more lost kids and take care of them. Overall 85 kids were supported. As part of our visit, below are the programs we conducted: 
1.	Books and stationery items for kids 
2.	Delicious food preparation and distribution 
3.	Sports item distribution like carrom board, volley ball etc. 
4.	Repairing electricity of NGO which helps day to day need of kids 
5.	Organized games and distributed prizes for the kids
