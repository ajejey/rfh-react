Activity# 1 : Distribution of books and laptop, projector to government school kids in Chikkaballapura

Date : July 27th 2024 

We from Rupee For Humanity thrive to provide education for all remote places in and around Bengaluru. This is way to support literacy and encourage education for all kids. As part of this vision, we performed below activities to government school in Chikkaballapura district. Overall 180 kids were supported with this cause. 

1.	Books distribution 
2.	Stationery items distribution 
3.	Donation of laptop and projector 
4.	Setup from normal classroom to digital classroom
5.	Lunch and snacks distribution
