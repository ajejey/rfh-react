Activity# 4 : RFH Juniors run 2024 

Date : Feb 11th 2024 
RFH conduced “Juniors run 2024” for the first time to raise funds for under-privileged kids. As one of our mission is to support under-privileged kids, we conducted this event for kids to participate in run and raise funds with that. Event was conducted in Dayananda Sagar college of Engineering, Bengaluru. 
With the funds raised, we sponsored education of under-privileged kids in and around Bengaluru.
